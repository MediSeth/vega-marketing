How to legally protect VEGA — the brand, the code, and everything you've built.

© Copyright AUTO-APPLIES
Protects: Source Code, Website, Bot Responses, Original Text

Copyright protects your original creative work — the bot's code, this website, your command responses, your documentation, and any original text you've written. In the US, copyright attaches automatically the moment you create it. You already own it.

However, registering with the US Copyright Office gives you stronger legal standing — it lets you sue for statutory damages (up to $150,000 per infringement) instead of just actual damages, and it creates a public record.

  • 01Go to copyright.gov/registration and create a free account
  • 02Choose "Register a Work" → Computer Program for the bot code
  • 03Choose Literary Work for your website text and documentation
  • 04Upload your source files or a representative portion (first + last 25 pages)
  • 05Pay the filing fee and receive your registration certificate (6–12 months)
ONLINE FILING FEE: $45–$65 (single work)
GROUP REGISTRATION: $85 (up to all bot versions)
TIMELINE: ~6–12 months for certificate
PROTECTION BEGINS: Day you created it
Add © 2025 VEGA. All rights reserved. to your site footer and bot embeds — this puts everyone on notice even before registration.
™ Trademark FILE WITH USPTO
Protects: "VEGA" Brand Name, Logo, Tagline, Bot Identity

A trademark protects your brand identity — the name "VEGA," your logo, and your tagline as they relate to Discord bots and gaming software. It prevents other people from launching a competing bot or product using your name.

File with the United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO) at teas.uspto.gov. You'll register under International Class 42 (Software as a Service) and optionally Class 41 (Entertainment/Gaming Services).

  • 01Search TESS (USPTO database) for "VEGA" in Class 42 to check for conflicts at tmsearch.uspto.gov
  • 02Go to teas.uspto.gov — use TEAS Plus for the lowest fee ($250/class)
  • 03Describe your goods/services: "AI-powered Discord bot providing gaming coaching, player statistics, and game tips"
  • 04Provide a specimen of use — a screenshot of VEGA in Discord or your website is sufficient
  • 05Track your application — USPTO reviews take ~8–12 months. You'll get a ™ immediately, ® after approval.
TEAS PLUS FEE: $250 per class (Class 42 = $250)
BOTH CLASSES: $500 total (recommended)

FAQ

QUESTIONS.

Is VEGA actually free — no tricks?+

Completely. The free tier never expires and doesn't require a payment method. You get AI coaching (10 queries/day), live stats for all 17 games, leaderboards, Steam linking, clans, and most commands. Premium adds unlimited coaching, daily DMs, screenshot roast, weekly recaps, and personal goals.

Which games can I look up stats for?+

All 17: League of Legends, Teamfight Tactics, Valorant, Apex Legends, Fortnite (Epic), Dota 2, Destiny 2, World of Warcraft, Counter-Strike 2, DayZ, Rust, PUBG, Team Fortress 2, Rocket League, Minecraft (Hypixel), and MTG Arena. AI coaching via /gg works for virtually any game including ones not listed.

Do I need a Steam account?+

No — Steam linking is completely optional. All commands work without it. Linking simply lets VEGA personalize coaching based on your actual library and hours instead of asking your game every time.

How does Server Premium work?+

Server Premium grants every current and future member of your server full Premium access automatically. You manage it from the /config control panel. When new members join, they're immediately Premium. One flat monthly price regardless of server size.

Can I cancel anytime?+

Yes. Monthly plans cancel through Gumroad anytime — you keep Premium until the end of your paid period. Lifetime purchases are permanent and require no subscription to maintain.

Is VEGA safe? Does it access my game accounts?+

VEGA only reads publicly available player stats from official game APIs. It never asks for passwords, login credentials, or personal data. Steam linking uses OAuth — you authorize through Steam's own secure page and VEGA only sees your Steam ID and public library. We never store passwords or account credentials.

What's the difference between /gg and /tipnow?+

/gg is open-ended — you ask any gaming question and get a full AI response. /tipnow delivers a single focused coaching tip targeted at your current skill level and game, similar to what you'd get from a daily tip DM. Both are free but /gg has a 10-query daily limit on the free tier.
